Pain Points to Probe

Discovery questions for Willie & Joe to prep talking points

Booking & Scheduling

How are they currently booking appointments? Phone only? Manual calendar management? Are they losing leads who call after hours?

Customer Follow-Up

Do they follow up with customers after service is complete? Is it manual or automated? How often do they ask for feedback?

Review Generation

Are they actively managing Google Reviews? Do they ask every happy customer to leave a review? What's their current star rating?

Missed Opportunities

What happens when they miss a call — do leads fall through the cracks? How do they handle after-hours inquiries?

Repeat Business

Are they tracking repeat customers? Do they run any loyalty or reactivation campaigns for customers who haven't been back in 6+ months?

Time Drain

How much time does Ava or her team spend on manual follow-ups, appointment confirmations, and review requests? Could that time be better spent?

Pitch Strategy

Keep it conversational, not salesy — Ava is already warm

The Approach

  • Lead with the car assessment: Willie is a customer first. Build the relationship during the service conversation.
  • Natural transition: "While I'm here, I wanted to share something we're building for local shops like yours..."
  • Demo the quick wins first: Missed-call text-back and booking automation — most immediately relatable for a shop owner.
  • Show, don't tell: Pull up the GHL demo account on mobile. Let Ava see the text-back in action.
  • Mention Gary: "My AI assistant has already been in contact with you via email — that's part of what we're building." Show the AI in action.
  • No pressure: "No obligation — just wanted to show you what's possible. If it makes sense, we can set up a proper demo."

Key Talking Points

  • "Most shops lose 30-40% of leads from missed calls. This eliminates that."
  • "Google Reviews are the #1 driver of new customers. We automate asking for them."
  • "Your best customers go dormant after 6 months. We bring them back automatically."
  • "You shouldn't be spending hours on follow-ups. Let AI handle it — you focus on wraps and wheels."
