The Problem

Running a brewery is a second job on top of your real job.

Inventory Chaos

Tracking hops, malt, yeast, CO2 manually. Running out or over-ordering. Waste eating margin. No system, just stress.

Batch Documentation

Fermentation logs, gravity readings, QC notes spread across notebooks, whiteboards, texts. Finding what you need is impossible.

Staff & Scheduling

Taproom shifts, brew days, events. Coordination overhead that pulls you away from brewing and serving customers.

Business Blind Spots

You're too busy operating to see what's actually driving revenue and what's draining it. Flying blind while running fast.

Ordering & Vendors

No system. Gut feel, last-minute emergency orders, inconsistent supplier relationships. It shouldn't be this hard.

It Never Stops

Owner-operators are always on. The business doesn't run without you in it. There's no off switch. No breathing room.

The Solution

We built the AI brain your brewery has been missing.

Operational Independence — Powered by AI

An intelligent operations layer that learns how Altamont runs and starts handling the complexity for you.

Smart Inventory

Knows what you have, what you need, and orders before you run out. Learns your usage patterns, tracks waste, optimizes ordering. No more emergency runs or over-ordering that ties up cash. Your AI knows when you're low before you do.

Brew Intelligence

Logs every batch automatically. Surfaces patterns. Catches problems early. Gravity readings, fermentation temps, tasting notes — all tracked, searchable, and analyzed. See what's working. Spot issues before they become losses. Your brewing knowledge, codified and amplified.

Staff Command Center

Scheduling, task assignment, shift coverage — handled. The AI knows who's good at what, who's available when, and what needs doing. No more group texts and scheduling chaos. Your team knows what to do. You get your time back.

Revenue Radar

Real-time view of what's selling, what's wasting, what's growing your margin. Which beers move. Which kegs sit. What events drive revenue. What's costing more than it makes. The data you need to make smart decisions, served up automatically.

Vendor Autopilot

Manages your supplier relationships and purchase orders intelligently. Tracks lead times, compares pricing, sends orders at the right time. Knows when to reorder, how much to order, and who to order from. Your supply chain, on cruise control.

The Daily Brief

Every morning: here's what needs your attention. Nothing more, nothing less. Low inventory? You'll know. Staff gaps? Covered. Batch ready to package? Flagged. Sales trending up on a specific beer? Highlighted. Only the signal. Zero noise.
