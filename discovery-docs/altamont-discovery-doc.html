5 Competitive & Market Context

▼

Livermore Craft Beer Market

  • Altamont Beer Works — West coast IPAs, hoppy styles, always fresh
  • Las Positas Brewing — Established local competitor
  • Working Man Brewing — Neighborhood brewery
  • Eight Bridges Brewing — Larger production facility
  • Market trend: Consumers want fresher beer, transparency, local sourcing

Industry Pain Points (Small Craft Breweries)

  • Inventory waste — hops/malt spoilage due to poor tracking
  • Batch tracking and QC documentation — manual spreadsheets prone to errors
  • Staff scheduling complexity — taproom hours, brew days, events
  • Taproom vs. distribution margin squeeze — where to focus?
  • Limited tech budgets — can't afford enterprise brewery software
  • Owner-operators doing everything — wearing too many hats
  • Data silos — POS, inventory, accounting don't talk to each other
