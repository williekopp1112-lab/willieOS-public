Gary's Take on energyandresources.ai

Unsolicited. Honest. Builder-to-builder.

Strengths

  • Sharp niche at the exact right time — AI + Energy is the $300B play over the next decade
  • "No fluff, no gatekeeping" ethos is the right cultural signal for the operator audience who's tired of hype
  • Real-time market data + curated AI news = genuine daily utility for decision-makers
  • Personal brand integration ("Jamian In The News") shows long game thinking — you're building authority, not just a news feed

Honest Feedback

  • The "Jamian In The News" section is empty — this is the trust anchor for new visitors. Fill it first with your Microsoft thought leadership, GP work, film credits.
  • Some AI news items in the feed may be AI-hallucinated or unverified (Gemini 3.0, GPT-5 energy launches that aren't confirmed) — add source verification or a "BETA" flag to protect credibility during early days
  • Missing: a clear "who is this for?" line above the fold. The hero says "Enterprise AI tools" but Jamian's personal brand is the draw — lean into that
  • Data source transparency: add an info button on the Market Pulse section explaining where data comes from (similar to Lou K.'s feedback in comments)

The Opportunity

The intersection of Jamian's AI workforce expertise (Microsoft, 124+ enterprise customers, $1B+ portfolio) + energyandresources.ai could become THE resource for operators figuring out what AI actually works in regulated industries — not theory, not hype — the real stuff from someone doing it at scale.
