What is WillieOS?

A personal AI operating system built on Claude. Willie built his own AI Chief of Staff named Gary Throttle that runs his life.

💡 The Vision

WillieOS is more than a productivity app — it's a complete AI-powered operating system for your life. Gary Throttle autonomously manages your calendar, email, tasks, notes, and research. You focus on what matters; Gary handles the rest.

🏗️ Architecture

  • React frontend with Zustand state management
  • MCP (Model Context Protocol) server for AI tools
  • Claude Sonnet 4.6 for intelligence
  • Turso (SQLite) for local-first data
  • Google Calendar & Gmail integrations
  • GitHub Pages for web publishing

How to Use WillieOS

Your quick-start guide to working with Gary

💬

Talk to Gary

Just type naturally. Gary figures out what tools to use. No commands, no syntax — just conversation.

✅

Manage Tasks (Loops)

"Add a task", "what do I need to do?", "mark X complete". Gary tracks your open loops and closes them.

📅

Calendar

"What's on my schedule?", "Book a meeting with X at 2pm Thursday". Gary syncs with Google Calendar.

📧

Email

"Check my inbox", "Draft a reply to Jake", "Send an email to...". Gary reads, writes, and sends emails.

🔍

Research & Docs

"Research X and write a report", "Build me a site about Y". Gary dispatches deep research and creates documents.

🤖

Dispatch Agents

Gary spins up Claude Code (for building) or Cowork (for documents) as sub-agents. Full autonomy.

⚡

Workflows

Automated playbooks: Morning Autopilot, Meeting Prep, Weekly Digest. Gary runs your life on autopilot.

🧠

Learn Your Preferences

Gary remembers how you like things done via teach_gary. The more you work with him, the better he gets.
